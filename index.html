Why this song?

⇌

This song 💕 is a heartfelt reminder that no matter how small you feel, you have something unique to offer the world. It is what makes you you—because even the smallest spark can light up the dark. When you feel like you're not good enough, not talented enough, not special enough, Kimi ni Dekiru Nanika teaches us that true value isn't found in being "the best," but in discovering what only we can do. Maybe you're not the top student or the most skilled employee, but there is a space in this world that only you can fill. Keep learning, keep growing, and trust that even the smallest ripples can create waves.

This song 🍃 paints the bittersweet picture of moving forward while carrying the memories of someone who is no longer by our side. It speaks to the quiet loneliness of change; the moment we step into a new chapter without the familiar voices that once guided us. Life doesn't always let us keep the people we rely on. There comes a time when we must stand on our own, whether it's leaving home for the first time, facing setbacks without our great mentor, or stepping into an unfamiliar future. Kimi ga inai Natsu reminds us that even when the road feels empty, we carry the lessons, the love, and the echoes of those who shaped us. Strength isn't found in the presence of comfort but in the courage to move forward despite its absence.
